Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ShiftPay Earnings handles information when you use our mobile application.

Important: ShiftPay Earnings does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share your personal data. All data you enter in the app is stored locally on your own device only.

1. About ShiftPay Earnings

ShiftPay Earnings is an app designed to help users estimate, track, and manage earnings related to shifts, hourly work, schedules, tips, bonuses, deductions, and similar income-related information.

2. No Data Collection

ShiftPay Earnings does not collect personal information from users. We do not operate a user account system, backend database, cloud sync, or server-side storage for your shift or earnings data.

We do not collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your employer information
  • Your shift data
  • Your earnings data
  • Your location
  • Your contacts
  • Your payment card details

3. Local Storage Only

Any information you enter into ShiftPay Earnings is stored locally on your device. This may include shift records, hourly rates, tips, bonuses, deductions, expenses, notes, settings, and calculated earnings.

This data is not sent to us and is not accessible by us. If you delete the app, reset your device, or manually remove app data, your locally stored information may be permanently deleted.

4. No Cloud Sync or Server Storage

ShiftPay Earnings does not currently provide cloud synchronization, online accounts, external backups, or server-side storage. Your data remains on your device unless you personally export, copy, screenshot, back up, or share it using your device’s own features.

5. How Your Data Is Used

Data entered into the app is used only on your device to provide app features, such as:

  • Calculating estimated earnings
  • Saving shift entries locally
  • Displaying work history
  • Managing app preferences
  • Estimating tips, bonuses, deductions, or expenses

6. Analytics and Tracking

ShiftPay Earnings does not use third-party advertising trackers or sell user data. If basic crash reporting or analytics are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated to clearly explain what is collected and why.

7. Purchases and App Store Payments

If ShiftPay Earnings offers paid features, one-time purchases, or subscriptions, payments are processed by the relevant app store provider, such as Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not directly collect, process, or store your payment card details.

Apple or Google may process limited purchase-related information according to their own privacy policies and terms.

8. Third-Party Services

ShiftPay Earnings does not send your shift or earnings data to third-party services. The app may rely on standard operating system services or app store services for basic functionality, installation, updates, purchases, or crash handling.

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, trade, or share your personal data. Because your shift and earnings data is stored only on your device, we do not have access to it.

10. Data Retention and Deletion

Your data remains on your device until you delete it inside the app, clear app data, uninstall the app, or reset your device. Since we do not store your data on our servers, we cannot recover deleted local data.

11. Backups

Depending on your device settings, your operating system may include app data in device backups, such as iCloud or Google device backups. These backups are controlled by you and by your device or operating system provider, not by ShiftPay Earnings.

12. Children’s Privacy

ShiftPay Earnings is not intended for children under the age of 13, or the minimum age required in your country. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

13. Security

Because your data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data depends partly on your device security settings. We recommend using a device passcode, biometric lock, and keeping your operating system up to date.

14. International Users

Since ShiftPay Earnings does not collect or store your personal data on our servers, most privacy requests related to access, correction, or deletion can be handled directly by managing the data on your own device. You can delete your app data by deleting entries in the app, clearing app data, or uninstalling the app.

15. Disclaimer About Earnings Calculations

ShiftPay Earnings provides estimates and tracking tools for informational purposes only. Calculations may not reflect final wages, taxes, deductions, employer adjustments, legal requirements, or official payroll records. Users should verify all earnings information with their employer, accountant, or official payroll provider.

16. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
